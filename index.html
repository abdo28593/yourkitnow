Questions Answered

Everything engineers need to know

Is this real KiCad? +
Yes. We use KiCad 6.0+ CLI with real EDA engines. Your designs are stored as native KiCad files (.kicad_pcb, .kicad_sch). You can open, edit, and extend them in the desktop app anytime.
Can I download and modify the files? +
Absolutely. You own 100% of your designs. Download Gerber, STEP, STL, and KiCad project files. Import into your CAD tools, send to manufacturers, or iterate further. No restrictions.
What about custom templates and design rules? +
Professional and Enterprise plans support custom templates. Set trace widths, via sizes, layer stackups, and routing constraints. We'll encode your design rules into the AI templates.
Does it handle complex designs? +
For simple to mid-range boards, yes. Multi-layer, analog, digital, and mixed-signal designs work well. High-speed, RF, and exotic topologies may require human review for optimal results.
How does the API work? +
POST your BOM or design spec as JSON. Get back a job ID. Poll for status or receive Telegram/webhook notifications. Download files via secure URL. Perfect for CI/CD pipelines and automation.
What formats do you support? +
Input: Text, BOM (CSV/JSON), schematic PDFs. Output: Gerber RS-274X, STEP 3D, STL, KiCad native files, PDF schematics, assembly drawings, and bill of materials.
Is my design data private? +
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't train models on your designs. After 30 days, files are auto-deleted unless you choose longer retention. Enterprise offers air-gapped options.
